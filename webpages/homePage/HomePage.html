About CommunityConnect:

We, the creators of community connect, strive to help people from different nationality's connect with others from the same race. In order to facilitate this, we have created an easy to use social media web interface to connect YOU to people in near your location. While we designed this to be for minorities, everyone is welcome to join the CC society.

How To Use CommunityConnect:

Using community connect is a very simple process. These 3 easy steps are as simple as:

  1. Creating a profile for yourself by clicking here

  2. After creating your profile, log in here

  3. Find your friends using the "search" button
